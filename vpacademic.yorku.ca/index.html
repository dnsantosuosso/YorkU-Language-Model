Building a Better Future in 2020-2025


The University Academic Plan: 2020-25 is about coming together to make positive change for our students, campuses and our local and global communities. York’s commitment to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (or SDGs) is essential to the University Academic Plan.

The Provost's Office works actively with the community to support these commitments. The Provostial Fellows Program is one way the Office builds capacity to advance the University Academic Plan and York's SDG Challenge.
