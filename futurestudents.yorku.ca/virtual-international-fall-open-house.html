Virtual International Fall Open House

Virtual International Open House at York

Join us on November 23, 2023 at 8:00 a.m. ET (see the time in your area)

Experience all that York has to offer as you discover our exciting undergraduate programs, diverse community and hands-on learning opportunities. 

This global event is designed to help you plan your next steps to begin undergraduate studies at YorkU, one of the most international universities in Canada. Join admission experts, academic representatives, international advisors and current students who will showcase unique opportunities for success at York University, and support your transition to our beautiful campuses in Toronto, Canada.

  • Explore academic programs that align with your goals
  • Learn about financing your education and planning your career
  • Discover the many ways York supports international students
  • Find out what you need to apply for admission
