Contact Information

We are currently experiencing a high volume of calls and e-mails (wait times are higher than usual). We thank you for your patience as we answer enquiries as quickly as we can.

For the fastest service at this time, please visit in person on Wednesdays between 10 am - 4 pm.

Notice: Fraudulent text messages have been circulating to students with a link to pay their deposits. Please note that York University does not solicit payments or send payment notices via SMS text messaging. Learn how to protect yourself from frauds and scams, and contact Admissions if you suspect fraudulent activity.

Warning about fraudulent offers of admission and scholarships

York University is aware that fraudulent third parties are misrepresenting themselves as agents, staff, or officers of the University and extending fraudulent offers of admission and scholarships to students. Protect yourself by understanding how York University does and does not communicate with students.

How to Apply

Our team of Student Support & Advising Representatives provide guidance and insight on the following:

  • Admission requirements
  • Academic upgrade for students who do not meet admission requirements
  • Application deadlines
  • Documentation required
  • Language proficiency requirements

Contact us via the methods listed below:

Drop-in support is now available for students seeking answers to Student Support & Advising services. Students will be seen on a first-come, first-served basis.

Note: For all OSAP inquiries, please ensure that you have uploaded your OSAP documents directly to your OSAP account.

How to use QLess:

QLess app
  1. Download the app on your smartphone from Google Play or the Apple App Store.
  2. Open the app and search for Office of the University Registrar (OUR) at York University.
  3. Choose the queue called Office of the University Registrar (OUR) - In-Person Service, Student Support and Advising.
  4. Enter your phone number where a SMS text message can be received.
  5. Once you are in the queue, you will receive an SMS text confirmation advising you to see one of our service desks. Completion of the YU Screening questionnaire is required.
Web portal

If you cannot access the QLess app, then you can also join the queue through this web portal.

  1. Enter your first and last name and your phone number where a SMS text message can be received. Click the Next button.
  2. Optional: Enter your York student ID number. Click the Next button.
  3. Choose the queue called Office of the University Registrar (OUR) - In-Person Service, Student Support and Advising.

How to use QLess:

QLess app
  1. Download the app on your smartphone from Google Play or the Apple App Store.
  2. Open the app and search for Office of the University Registrar (OUR) at York University.
  3. Choose the queue called Office of the University Registrar (OUR) - Zoom - Virtual Service, Student Support and Advising.
  4. Enter your phone number.
  5. You will receive a text message (not an email) with a Zoom ID and password.
  6. Go to zoom.us/join and enter that Zoom ID and password to access the video call.
Web portal

If you cannot access the QLess app, then you can also join the queue through this web portal.

  1. Enter your first and last name and your phone number where a SMS text message can be received. Click the Next button.
  2. Optional: Enter your York student ID number. Click the Next button.
  3. Choose the queue called Zoom - Virtual Service, Student Support and Advising.
  4. You will receive a text message (not an email) with a Zoom ID and password.
  5. Go to zoom.us/join and enter that Zoom ID and password to access the video call.
