Faculty of Graduate Studies Domestic Graduate Admissions at York
Mon, Dec 11, 2023
5 - 6 p.m. ET (see time in your city)

We invite you to join us for a Graduate Admissions Session for Domestic Applicants - learn more about York University and our graduate admissions process. This session is intended for applicants who are Canadian citizens or permanent residents.

 

This session will run about an hour, and will cover "Why York", eligibility, requirements, financial information, how to apply, and more.

 

Please note that during these sessions we will not be able to answer any questions about Schulich School of Business programs, or Osgoode Law School programs. For all other master's programs, doctoral programs, and graduate diplomas, this is the session for you!

How to Apply to the BSW Program as a Transfer or Mature Student
Tue, Jan 16, 2024
12 - 1 p.m. ET (see time in your city)

Thank you for your interest in joining us at York University! This is your chance to learn more about how to apply to the Bachelor of Social Work (BSW) as a Transfer or as a Mature Student. This is a collaborative session led by Transfer and Mature Recruitment Officer, Krenare Aliovski, and the School of Social Work part of the Faculty of Liberal Arts & Professional Studies at York University. In this virtual webinar, you will learn: • How to prepare your application to the BSW program • Guidance and Tips for completing your Supplementary Form • Applying as a Transfer or Mature student; • Transfer Credit Process; • Support Services; • Scholarships & Bursaries; • Process/timing of offers and Next Steps; We'll also be sure to leave lot of time for questions and answers. We look forward to meeting you online!
