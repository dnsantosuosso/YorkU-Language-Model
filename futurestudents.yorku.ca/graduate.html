Graduate Student

 

Whether you seek a competitive professional edge or love research, our graduate programs help you to build your skills, sharpen your insights and make an impact. Benefit from strong academic and financial support and connections with helpful mentors as you grow as a leader, scholar, researcher, artist or teacher.

Ever wonder if there is a bachelor program can allow one to discover their own artistic journey? School of the Arts, Media, Performance & Design
Audition Monologue Workshop for Theatre - York University (Toronto, Canada)
Fri, Dec 8, 2023 8 - 9 a.m.

In this Audition Monologue Workshop led by Jamie Robinson - a versatile artist and professor at York University, participants will explore how to prepare for an Acting audition, which is an essential part of the actor’s craft.

The workshop will explore: (1) Breaking down text; (2) Personalizing text; and (3) Audition technique.

Participants should come with a short monologue from a play (2 minutes or less) that they’re ready to explore.
