Work/Study Programs

The Work/Study Program at York University provides eligible undergraduate students (domestic and international) with the opportunity to develop professional skills and contribute to the university through paid, on-campus employment opportunities.

Financial Advising

Learning to manage money is not an easy task, especially for students, whose resources are often limited. Set up your multi-purpose online application form used for many of York's scholarships and awards.

Refunds

Students can request refunds if there is a credit balance on their student record, or if you've dropped a course (or courses) before the drop deadline date. In other cases, you may also petition for a refund in extenuating circumstances.
