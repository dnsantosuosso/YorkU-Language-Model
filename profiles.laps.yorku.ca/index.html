Welcome to Faculty Profiles

The Faculty of Liberal Arts & Professional Studies is home to over 650 full-time faculty known for teaching and research excellence in an array of subjects from the social sciences, the humanities and professional programs. Engaged in collaborative projects at home and abroad, LA&PS faculty are active in more than 80 countries.

Explore the depth and breadth of research in LA&PS through our faculty profiles. The profiles provide information on professors’ past and present research and academic expertise, as well as contact information. Search the database of profiles by last name or by department and school. Alternatively, enter relevant key words into the search field for more specialized results.
