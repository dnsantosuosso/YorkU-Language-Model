Student Planner

Find monthly deadlines and other important Financial and Academic information below. 

September

✔️   Make sure you’ve got all the important Fall/Winter session dates and deadlines marked down! 

  • Undergraduate Important Dates
  • Graduate Studies Important Dates
  • Course Fee Refund Deadlines
  • OSAP Important Dates and Deadlines

✔️   Check your student health plan coverage, opt-out options and deadlines 

         NOTE: You will see your health plan(s) charged to your online student account 

October

Checking in: 

  • How are you doing in your courses? 
  • Did you pay your fees in full? 
  • Revisit your enrolment plan and budget 

    November

    ✔️   Drop courses if needed 

    ✔️   Update  your Fall/Winter government financial aid application 

    • RESOURCE: Reporting changes to your OSAP application info 

     

    December

    ✔️   Check final Fall grades 

    ✔️   Check your December bill to confirm the minimum payment due January 10th 

    • DEEPER DIVE: Your student account 

    ✔️   Confirm the course refund deadlines (BEFORE your drop or change courses!) 

    January

    ✔️   Make sure you’ve got all the important Winter term dates/deadlines marked down!

    • Undergraduate Important Dates
    • Graduate Studies Important Dates
    • Course Fee Refund Deadlines
    • OSAP Important Dates and Deadlines

    ✔️   Book your enrolment planning appointment with an advisor  for February 

    February

    ✔️   Start planning for summer 

    • RESOURCE: Enrolment guide 

    ✔️   Update your Fall/Winter government  financial aid application 

    • RESOURCE: Reporting changes to your OSAP application info 

     

    March

    ✔️   Download your tuition tax form and submit your income tax return 

    • RESOURCE: Tax Forms and Resources to learn about income taxes in Canada  

    ✔️   Enrol in Summer courses and double-check your timetable 

    • RESOURCE: Enrolment guide 

    ✔️   Apply for government financial aid for the Summer term 

    • RESOURCE: Summer OSAP 

     

    April

    ✔️   Start planning for next Fall/Winter 

    • RESOURCE: Enrolment guide 

    ✔️   Fill out your Summer Student Financial Profile 

    ✔️   Check your April bill to confirm the minimum payment due May 10th 

    • DEEPER DIVE: Your student account 

    ✔️   Confirm Summer course refund deadlines 

     

    May

    ✔️   Make sure you’ve got all the important Summer term dates/deadlines marked down!

    • Undergraduate Important Dates
    • Graduate Studies Important Dates
    • Course Fee Refund Deadlines
    • OSAP Important Dates and Deadlines

    ✔️   Check your final Fall/Winter grades and academic decision 

    June

    ✔️   Enrol in courses and double-check your timetable 

    • RESOURCE: Enrolment guide 
    • DEEPER DIVE: Undergraduate calendar (to find your degree, program and/or certificate requirements) 

    ✔️   Apply for your Fall/Winter government financial aid and submit any required documents 

    • RESOURCE: OSAP application guide 

    July

    ✔️   Update your Summer government financial aid and/or Student Financial Profile apps 

    • RESOURCE: Reporting changes to your OSAP application info 

    ✔️   Review your budget (do you have enough resources for your upcoming year?) 

    • RESOURCE: Financial Planning 

    ✔️   Confirm Fall/Winter course fees 

    • DEEPER DIVE: Academic Tuition Fee Categories 

     

    August

    ✔️   Check your final SU grades and academic decision 

    ✔️   Check your August bill to confirm the minimum payment due September 10th 

             DEEPER DIVE: Your student account 

    ✔️   Confirm the  course refund deadlines (BEFORE you drop or change courses!) 

    ✔️   Submit  your Fall/Winter Student Financial Profile to be considered for awards.
