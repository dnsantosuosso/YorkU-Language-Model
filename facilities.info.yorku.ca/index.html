Facilities Services

Facilities Services is a team of professionals with a disciplined focus on efficient, sustainable and effective delivery of support services for the University. We are committed to the design, construction, maintenance and utilities of the buildings and grounds. Customer service and satisfaction are of paramount concern within our operations.
