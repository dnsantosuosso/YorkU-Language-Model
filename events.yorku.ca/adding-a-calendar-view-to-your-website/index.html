Adding a calendar view to your website

York’s Central Events Calendar makes it possible for you to post events to our central system and display them locally on a York departmental site. You can add the entire York events calendar to your site or a custom view of relevant events.

How it works

Please fill out the form below to request a code for your site. The following information must be provided:

  1. A primary contact name and email for your site events
  2. Your website address
  3. A list of categories that should be including in the feed

Once created, we will send you a simple 1-line Javascript snippet that can be embedded on your events page. This can be done by editing your page, adding a custom HTML block and pasting in the provided code.
