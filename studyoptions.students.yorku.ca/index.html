Study Options - Home

At York University, we encourage you to take advantage of our flexible study options. Map out what, when, where and how you want to study.
Your degree, your choice.

For York Students
Study at Another Institution
York partners with numerous Canadian and international academic institutions so that our students can study abroad or locally and build relationships with visiting students.
Opportunities Beyond the Classroom
Take what you learn in the classroom and apply that knowledge to practical scenarios. Gain competitive industry experience and develop concrete skills while pursuing your degree.
Full-time, Part-time & More
Do you have work, family or other personal commitments to balance outside of York? We get it; which is why we offer flexible study options including online courses (eLearning), evening and weekend classes, and part-time studies. Design a class schedule that works for you.
For Incoming Students
Pathways to a York Credential

We offer career-focused academic pathways and programs that recognize previous postsecondary studies, allow you to study with one of our partner institutions or support your transition between programs.
For Former York Students
Return to York

Have you taken a break from your studies and now wish to return? By meeting a few conditions, you should be able to continue your studies with us.

For York Graduates
Post-Graduation Planning
After you graduate, you may change your mind about your career aspirations or plan on enhancing your degree with additional qualifications. York offers a variety of options for professional development as well as different ways for you to continue your education to prepare you for your career.
