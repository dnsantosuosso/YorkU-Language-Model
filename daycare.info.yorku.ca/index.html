The York University Co-operative Daycare Centre (YUCDC) provides a caring, educational, and multicultural daycare environment on York University's Keele campus. We've served the York University community since 1969 as a non-profit, cooperative organization.

Many of our parents are students, staff and faculty of York University. Although priority is given to the York University Community, YUCDC also accepts children from outside the University Community.
