Welcome to the Office of Advising and Integrated Student Services – Supporting student success from admission to graduation.

Supporting student success from admission to graduation.

OAISS is your destination for undergraduate advising, resources, workshops and information to support you throughout your degree completion.

Step 1: Do you have questions regarding any of the following:

  • Registrar & Financial Services
  • Campus Life & Events
  • Career Advising Information
  • Mental Health & Wellness Resources

If so, please Ask SAVY, who is ready to help you get more connected to resources for academic success, health & wellness, student life and career development! You can access this virtual tool by logging into your moodle account!


Step 2: OAISS will be conducting appointments either in-person or virtually. When booking an appointment, please indicate on the online form your preference. Please refer to your email, where we will confirm how your appointment will be delivered.

To schedule an appointment, please complete this Advising Appointment Booking Form. Students are always offered the earliest available advising time slot at the time of their request; unless a previously booked appointment cancels or reschedules, we cannot offer an earlier date and time. We will endeavour to respond to you as soon as we can. Thank you for your understanding.

Step 3: Meet your advisor!
Please arrive on time and if possible, bring along your previous degree worksheet. If you are not able to make your appointment and need to reschedule, please email oaiss@yorku.ca. Our office is located on the 3rd floor of the Goldfarb Centre (follow the red footsteps!), Monday to Thursday, 9:00AM-4:00PM, and Friday from 9:00AM- 3:00PM *Please note the office is closed between 12-1pm daily for lunch.
