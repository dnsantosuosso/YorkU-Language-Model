Artstor Digital Library This link opens in a new window
Popular
A repository of images from hundreds of museums, artists, artists' estates, photographers, scholars, special collections, and photo archives from around the world. See Guide & What's new!!
EBSCO Databases This link opens in a new window
Popular
EBSCOhost web gives access to several academic databases including Anthropology Plus, Applied Science & Technology, CINAHL, General Science Abstracts, Historical Abstracts, SportDiscus and many more. See Ebsco Tutorials.
Creating Permalinks to articles in EBSCO databases.
JSTOR This link opens in a new window
Popular
JSTOR provides access to more than 12 million academic journal articles, books and primary sources in 75 disciplines. See JSTOR Help page for more tutorials.
You can also search the captions of images within items in the database. To search for content with images use the “caption” option on the drop-down menu on the Advanced Search page. Creating permalinks to articles in JSTOR
ProQuest Central This link opens in a new window
Popular
Alternate Name(s) ProQuest ProQuest Databases
ProQuest databases provides a single source for scholarly journals, newspapers, reports, working papers, and datasets along with millions of pages of digitized historical primary sources including ebooks. See ProQuest LibGuide. See also ProQuest Platform training
ProQuest eBook Central This link opens in a new window
Popular
ProQuest Ebook Central is an important and multidisciplinary resource for e-books and provides breadth and depth in terms of authoritative e-content from a number of recognized publishers. Check out the the YUL EBbooks LibGuide. Learn more about this e-book platform.
Explore ProQuest e-book Central LibGuide.
PubMed This link opens in a new window
Popular
PubMed is a free search engine accessing primarily the MEDLINE database of references and abstracts on life sciences and biomedical topics. The United States National Library of Medicine (NLM) at the National Institutes of Health is responsible for maintaining this database. See tutorials. Non-York users, Open Access PubMed available from this link
Creating Permalinks to an article in PubMed. The YUL customized PubMed link provides access to full-text articles and e-journals subscribed by York Libraries.
Scholars Portal ebooks This link opens in a new window
Popular
Alternate Name(s) E-Books at Scholars Portal
Scholars Portal Books provides access to a wide range of scholarly digital texts on a single platform. Most of the content is from academic publishers, and is available to Ontario universities that have purchased ebooks with perpetual access rights. For more information see YUL EBbooks LibGuide
Creating permalinks for Scholars Portal e-books
Scholars Portal Journals This link opens in a new window
Popular
Alternate Name(s) Scholars Portal ejournals, Ejournals at scholars portal
Scholars Portal is a repository of over 40 million scholarly articles drawn from journals covering every academic discipline. Most hosted journals are subscription-based and are available only to Ontario universities who have negotiated access for them. A small but growing number of journals are open access.
Scopus This link opens in a new window
Popular
Scopus is one of the the world’s largest abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed literature, including scientific journals, books and conference proceedings, covering research topics across all scientific and technical disciplines, ranging from medicine and social sciences to arts and humanities. Further, with smart tools to track, analyze and visualize research, Scopus empowers you to advance your science beyond the text. For tutorials see Scopus Help.
Web of Science This link opens in a new window
Popular
Web of Science is a platform consisting of several literature search databases designed to support scientific and scholarly research. See WoS libguide & training
Web of Science Core Collection includes over 20,000 peer-reviewed, high-quality scholarly journals published worldwide (including Open Access journals); over 190,000 conference proceedings; and over 90,000 editorially selected books. More information
Wiley Online This link opens in a new window
Popular
Alternate Name(s) Wiley
Wiley Online Library features 1,600 journals and over 21,000 online books, hundreds of multi-volume reference works and other resources. Some subjects covered: Agriculture, Architecture, Business, Chemistry, Humanities, Law and Psychology.
York University Libraries does not subscribe to all content (full-text) in Wiley. In case there is a particular article indexed in Wiley Online, and we do not have access to it - please use RACER ILL to request that article.

New
Alternate Name(s) Maritime & Commercial Module (i-law)
