What we do

The Library Data Services team is here to help York students and faculty find and use the data and statistics they need for their research. To seek assistance, check out our virtual consultation appointment calendar or contact us at datalib@yorku.ca.

Please note that research data management assistance is also available remotely for those looking to manage and publish their research data. To seek assistance, please contact yul_rdm@yorku.ca.

For specialized data support in the context of a course or research project, contact a Library Data Services team member from the list provided below.

We have a team of librarians (Rosa Orlandini, Xuemei Li, and Alexandra Wong) at York University Libraries that can help you find the numeric data and/or statistics you need to complete an assignment or further your research. We are available to provide classroom instruction about data & statistics resources in general or in specific subject areas, as well as data literacy. For more information, consult the Data & Statistics Research Guide or email datalib@yorku.ca.

 

Visit the Geospatial Data Research Guide and Maps and Atlases Research Guide to learn more about available services, resources, and collections. 

Visit the Subject Guide for Finance to learn more about available resources and services.

Minglu Wang is the Research Data Management (RDM) Librarian at York University. She provides research data management training and consultation services to the York research community. If you need assistance with writing / reviewing a data management plan (DMP) or depositing research data to York University Dataverse, please contact yul_rdm@yorku.ca. 

Alexandra Wong is the Data Visualization and Analytics Librarian at York University. She can provide support through training and consultations on topics related to data visualization, such as how to choose the best data visualization, what tool to use, and more. Visit the Data Visualization Research Guide to learn more about available resources and services. She can also assist the York community with their data research needs.

Data Services Consultation Appointments

Students and faculty requiring specialized assistance with finding data for their research or assignments can schedule a consultation with a data librarian. 

Other data-related services on campus

  • Statistical Consulting Service ISR’s Statistical Consulting Service (SCS), made up of faculty members, graduate students and ISR staff, provides consultation on a broad range of problems in data analysis, research design and statistical computing, concentrating primarily on social science research. Consultation is without charge to the York community. SCS also offers short courses on various aspects of statistics and statistical computing. SCS regularly teaches introductions to the SPSS and SAS packages,
  • University Information Technology Contact the UIT Helpdesk for assistance with: software installation and licence key problems (e.g. SPSS and SAS); data portability between applications (e.g. SPSS and SAS); data portability between hardware platforms; questions concerning the use of SPSS, SAS, or other statistical software; information about setting up a computing account; assistance with hardware specifications and configurations
  • Research Data Centre The York RDC provides social science, health and policy researchers access to analyze Statistics Canada’s master data sets. In the RDC’s secure environment researchers can access surveys in full detail, without the removal of geographic and other information required to protect respondent confidentiality in the public use data files.
  • Office of Institutional Planning & Analysis Provideds access to a variety of datasets about York.
