How to Trace Provincial Legislation, Part 1

These three short videos will walk you through the process of tracing Ontario legislation. They will show you how to trace most legislation but, from time to time, there will exceptions to the rules. If you are stuck, contact a librarian for assistance.

Getting Started

This video covers the types of bills, how they are numbered, and the difference between short and long titles.

How to Trace Provincial Legislation, Part 2

In this video, you will be introduced to the legislative process in Ontario. We'll look at the way a bill normally passes in the Legislative Assembly.

How to Trace Provincial Legislation, Part 3

This video will take you through the process of tracing a bill using the Legislative Assembly website.
