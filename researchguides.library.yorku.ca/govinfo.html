Introduction

York University Library collects and makes accessible information produced by a variety of governments, both in Canada and abroad. This Guide will assist you in finding the Library's specialized Guides. Each of the tabs will lead you to Guides covering: 

  • Canada: Guides to the Canadian Federal Government, Ontario government, other Provinces and Territories, the City of Toronto, and other Canadian Municipalities 
  • Foreign: Guides to information produced by foreign countries
  • International: Guides to information produced by International Government Organizations, for instance, the United Nations, the World Bank, the European Union, etc.
  • Topical: Multi-jurisdictional guides on specific topics. 
  • Instructional Videos: How to videos for conducting research using government information.
  • Citing Government Information: How to cite government information in APA, MLA, Chicago, and other styles
