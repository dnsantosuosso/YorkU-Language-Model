Research Assistance

Do you need assistance in finding maps, atlases, airphotos, scanned maps or other cartographic materials for your assignment or research project?  You email your questions or book an appointment at gislib@yorku.ca. 

What's in this guide

The purpose of this guide is to provide you with tools and strategies to locate maps for your assignment and or for your research. You can browse this guide by looking up the type of map or by topic. Each section of this guide will highlight online and print map and atlas resources at York University Libraries and beyond.
