Alternative Dispute Resolution

Conflict is a natural part of the world around us and we expect that from time to time community members will encounter conflict in many different ways.  Many disputes may be resolved without resorting to a formal Complaint process.  Our office focuses on assisting York community members resolve their conflicts with students using cooperative problem-solving methods.

In a situation where a York community member is experiencing a conflict with a student, an OSCR advisor may suggest possible approaches to resolving the problem.

If you require assistance, please contact us at: oscr@yorku.ca.
