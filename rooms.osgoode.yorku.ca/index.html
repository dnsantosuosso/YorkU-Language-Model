Osgoode Hall Law School Spaces

Reserve a space at Osgoode Hall Law School for studying, meditation, or group meetings

The Osgoode Hall Law School Library offers reservable Study Rooms for currently enrolled Osgoode students for individual or group studying or for attending classes remotely while in our building. 

Osgoode Hall Law School Library

  • Law Library Group Study Rooms are for currently enrolled Osgoode Hall Law School students only
  • Students can book 1 week in advance and only for 3 hours per student per day. 
  • Room bookings MUST be made through the online booking system LibCal here
  • You will receive a reservation confirmation to your Osgoode email address. You are required to confirm your booking
  • Please have your YU-card and booking confirmation email ready to show to a library staff member when showing up to the library to sign out the key for the room
  • Students will be charged a late fee for not returning the study room key when it is due back at the circulation desk
  • The Osgoode Law Library is not responsible for lost or missing personal items. Please do not leave your belongings unattended when using the Law Library.
  • Questions about confirmed bookings can be sent to library@osgoode.yorku.ca. 

Student Wellness Suite & Reflection Room

The Student Wellness Suite is intended as a quiet space for students to relax, meditate, and seek calm in the midst of daily stress.

  • Access to this Suite is for currently enrolled Osgoode Hall Law School students
  • Suite bookings MUST be made through the online booking system LibCal here: Student Wellness Suite
  • You will receive a reservation confirmation to your Osgoode email address. You are required to confirm your booking.
  • Please have your YU-card and booking confirmation email ready to show to a library staff member when showing up to the library to sign out the key for the suite. 
  • Questions about confirmed bookings can be sent to library@osgoode.yorku.ca. 

Osgoode Hall Law School’s Reflection Room (Room 0005) is intended as a quiet space for Osgoode students for the practice of their faith, reflection, and prayer.

  • Access to this Suite is for currently enrolled Osgoode Hall Law School students
  • Suite bookings MUST be made through the online booking system LibCal here: Reflection Room
  • You will receive a reservation confirmation to your Osgoode email address. You are required to confirm your booking.
  • Questions about confirmed bookings can be sent to  rooms@osgoode.yorku.ca.

Attending Remote Classes and Group Study Spaces

Helliwell Centre & Room 0014K

This space is intended for students to use for remote class participation. There are 6 private rooms available to book for 1 individual or a small group (see max. capacity) attending the same remote class. Please note that on occasion, a student's booking in the Helliwell Centre may be rescheduled to another room based on the needs of the Law School.

Small Group or Remote Learning Space

This space is intended for small student group meetings or remote class participation.

  • Reservations MUST be made through the online booking system LibCal here.
  • You can request up to 3 hours per day; booking availability is limited to the next 14 days.
  • You will receive a reservation confirmation to your Osgoode email address; you are required to confirm your booking.
  • Each person must have their room email confirmation ready when they arrive for the booking.
  • Questions about confirmed bookings can be sent to rooms@osgoode.yorku.ca.
