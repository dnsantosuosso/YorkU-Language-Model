Share your story with YFile

Do you have a story to share with the York community? Want to promote an upcoming event? Highlight your current research? Share recent innovations in teaching and learning? Tell us about an exciting University-community initiative or an outstanding faculty/staff member or student? YFile is always looking for unique story ideas.

Things to consider:

  • Why will your story be of interest to the York community?
  • What’s the news hook?
  • Does your story promote or support the University’s strategic priorities?
  • What makes the story compelling or unique?
  • Is your news tip or story idea time sensitive?

Please be as thorough as possible when completing the form. We look forward to hearing from you!

Time estimated to complete this form: less than five minutes (source: Google Analytics, Nov. 2, 2022)

NOTE: If you do not receive a confirmation after submitting your story, please email yfile@yorku.ca.
