Equipment Loans

Loan Process

  1. Become a member by taking our orientation course.
  2. Use the Book buttons to borrow equipment for up to 7 calendar days.
  3. Wait for email that confirms your booking (5 min).
  4. Bring your YU-card to Scott Library Room 207 on the first day of the loan period (otherwise the loan is cancelled).
  5. Meet staff who examine the reserved equipment with you (15 min).
  6. Create with curiosity and responsibility!
  7. Bring the equipment yourself to Room 207 during the MCL's hours by the due date.
  8. Meet staff who examine the returned equipment with you (15 min).

Users must follow our booking policy and safer space policy. For Glendon students, visit the Glendon Digital Media Lab!

The MCL is currently in the process of piloting its workflows and processes with a limited group of partners. We will be working to expand our offerings to a broader set of partners as our pilot progresses. Your feedback is important to us. Please contact diginit@yorku.ca for more information, or fill out our pilot feedback form.
