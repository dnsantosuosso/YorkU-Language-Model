Door Access Card Registration
 

Registering your door access cards

The Door Access Card Program allows students to gain entry into rooms such as labs, that are controlled by a card reader for security reasons. Students will not be able to access these rooms without a door access card. At the time of receipt the door access card is not active and does not contain any access privileges. For more information on Door Access Cards, please visit the Security Services website.
