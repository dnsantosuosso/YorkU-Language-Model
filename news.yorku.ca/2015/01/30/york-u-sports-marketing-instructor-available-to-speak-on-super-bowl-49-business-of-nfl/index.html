York U sports marketing instructor available to speak on Super Bowl 49 & business of NFL

January 30, 2015Advisory, Super Bowl 49suhasini

TORONTO, January 30, 2015 – With the Super Bowl 49 this weekend, and broadcast regulator CRTC’s announcement Thursday to allow Canadians to see the much-anticipated American television ads starting 2017, excitement has heightened.

York U Sports Marketing Instructor Vijay Setlur is available for media interviews to discuss various aspects of marketing and advertising surrounding the National Football League (NFL), leading up to its Super Bowl championship game on Sunday, February 1:

  • NFL's brand image amid the recent domestic abuse scandals
  • recent partnership with YouTube
  • impact on athlete brands and marketing
