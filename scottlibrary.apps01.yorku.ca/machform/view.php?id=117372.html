YorkSpace Mediated Deposit Submission Form

The YorkSpace mediated deposit service is open to York University staff, faculty, librarians, and affiliated researchers and is designed to support the aims of the York University Senate Policy on Open Access.

Please note that the deposit service is primarily designed to support researchers needing to deposit copies of peer-reviewed scholarly articles, book chapters, or conference papers. To discuss the deposit of other types of research objects, please contact opendeposit@yorku.ca.

The Libraries will contact you with information on next steps once your form is submitted.
