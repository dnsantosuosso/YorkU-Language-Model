Appointments for Non-Loanable CDs and DVDs

Make an appointment to view or listen to a non-loanable DVD or CD in the Scott Library.

  • Book an Appointment

    If you wish to view or listen to a Sound and Moving Image Collection DVD or CD that you cannot borrow, please make an appointment to listen or view the resources in the Scott library.

    Appointments are generally available Monday to Friday, between 9am and 3pm. Two business days notice is required.

    Available to students, staff, faculty, alumni and other community users. Please bring photo ID and your own headphones.

    Library staff will be in touch within the next business day to confirm your appointment.
