Winter Career Fair 2024

Winter Career Fair 2024

Thursday, Jan 18.

Time: 10 a.m. - 3 p.m.

Location: Victor Phillip Dahdaleh Building

Meet Employers who are looking to hire York U students for a variety of opportunities,
including part-time, summer, internship, and full-time positions after graduation.

The University encourages community members to wear a mask in indoor spaces, especially where physical distancing is not possible. Everyones choice to continue masking based on comfort levels and health needs should be respected.
