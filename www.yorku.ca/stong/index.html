Welcome to Stong College

Stong College is affiliated with the Faculty of Health, serving students from Kinesiology & Health Science, and Nursing. Enriched by the Stong family pioneer spirit, to which we owe our name, our community is committed to innovation while preserving the values of diversity and inclusivity.

Our team at Stong College aims to support student success through a variety of programs designed for students throughout their university experience.
