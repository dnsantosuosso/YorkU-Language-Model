Are you a York University employee? 
Please visit www.yorku.ca/yuhire to view and apply for jobs
through the Employee Career Portal – YU Hire.

York University employees must apply to jobs through the Employee Career Portal (YU Hire).
If you are a current York University employee and/or are using your rights under a collective
agreement to view and/or apply to jobs, you MUST log into YU Hire to access the
York University Employee Career Portal.
