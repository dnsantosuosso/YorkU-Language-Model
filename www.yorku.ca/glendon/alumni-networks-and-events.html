Keeping alumni connected to each other and to Glendon is top of mind. Networks are more than Glendon Welcome Wagons, We aim to provide social connections, meaningful networking and a positive connection to Glendon today. Networks can support student recruitment, exchanges, volunteering, student work opportunities and more. Glendon Networks Interested in events, both on and off campus? Consult our Glendon Alumni Calendar

We are incredibly grateful to our volunteers who devote time and expertise to lead our Glendon Networks.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have had to adapt to this special situation while maintaining the unique ties that connect Glendon and our alumni community. More than ever, we need our networks that contribute to Glendon’s standing.

Until further notice from Public Health, our events remain online. Our calendar of events is up to date.

Toronto

A working group has been established to provide feedback and support for the continued growth of Glendon alumni programs. 

For this initiative, representatives across the spectrum of our fifty years of graduates were selected. If you are Interested in supporting this initiative, please contact Mireille Millette at 416-487-6801 or mireille.millette@glendon.yorku.ca
