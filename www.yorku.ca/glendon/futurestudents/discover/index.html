Glendon and York’s Schulich School of Business offer a combined 4+1 Pathway to Master of Management (MMgt). Build on your four-year Glendon undergrad program of choice with the one-year MMgt degree, which gives you a solid base in management fundamentals.

With French Studies, Translation or Linguistics and Language Studies you can fast-track towards a career in Interpretation or Speech Language Pathology. Glendon alumna Malikah is an English-French interpreter based in Paris who has made her mark in a challenging European market.

Complete two university degrees at the same time to become a qualified and sought after teacher in Ontario with our Concurrent Bachelor of Education. The concentration in French as a Second Language is in high demand and prepares you to teach in French immersion, Core and Extended French classrooms at all certification levels.
