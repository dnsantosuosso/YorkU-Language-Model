Cycling

York University encourages and supports cycling as a sustainable transportation alternative.

Secured Bike Enclosures

York Universityâ€™s Keele Campus offers two (2) secured bike enclosures for use by community members.  They are conveniently located at either end of the campus, one is in the Arboretum Lane Parking Garage, the other enclosure is in the Student Services Parking Garage.

These enclosures offer protection against theft, vandalism, and inclement weather.  They are monitored 24/7 by CCTV and are only accessible to community members that are registered for use and have key access.

They are located on street level just inside the main entrances to each garage for easy access. There is a fee of $20 + applicable taxes per year (May 1 - April 30) to use these bike enclosures. This fee will not be pro-rated and goes towards supporting improvements and upkeep.

Please contact the Parking Office for additional information.

Secured Bike Enclosure Application

Completed applications must be submitted via email to parking@yorku.ca.  A member of the Parking Services team will contact you to complete the application process and arrange for key pick up.

Bike Share Toronto

York University is the first University in the Greater Toronto Area to partner with Bike Share Toronto to provide yet another sustainable mode of transportation to its community members. Bike Share Toronto stations can be found on the Keele and Glendon campuses. Etiquette

Campus walkways are shared-use zones.  Cyclists must show courtesy to pedestrians at all times, especially in high traffic areas.

Regenesis Cycle York

Regenesis Cycle York is a community, do-it-yourself bike centre located at York University Keele Campus. We offer affordable repairs, bikes, regular educational workshops, group rides and other events to encourage more ridership on campus. To stay up to date with Cycle York news you can follow us on Instagram @cycleyork_regenesis and for any enquiries feel free to reach out to us at cycleyork@regenesis.eco.
