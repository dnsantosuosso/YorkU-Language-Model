FUTURE STUDENTS OF YORK UNIVERSITY CAN LOG IN HERE TO MANAGE APPLICATION SERVICES

Undergraduate students (including Education):
  • check your application status (and accept your offer if you've been admitted)
  • upload required documents (where applicable)
  • complete a supplementary information and/or referee form (Education; Schulich School of Business; Social Work, except Canadian high-school applicants)
  • book your enrolment appointment
  • complete your student financial profile
  • apply for residence
  • view your address details
Graduate students:
  • check your application status (and accept your offer if you've been admitted)
  • complete a supplementary information and/or referee form (for select programs only; see your program page for details)
  • apply for apartment housing
  • upload required documents (where applicable)
  • view your address details
It will take a minimum of four business days to process your application. You will not be able to log into MyFile until that period has passed.
