Services Pathways

Learning Skills Services Workshops

We offer online and in-person workshops on all the skills you need to achieve your academic goals. With many topics to choose from, our workshops are open to all students at any level and are designed to help you learn how to manage your time, study and learn more effectively, keep up with readings and course work, and get the most out of your studies.

Register for a Workshop

Learning Skills Drop-In

Visit us and speak with one of our friendly Learning Skills Peers about learning skills-related strategies to support your academic success. Topics range from how to develop and stick to a realistic time management plan to how to tackle your readings, upcoming assignments and exams.

Learn more about learning skills drop-in

Learning Commons

The Learning Commons at York is a collaborative partnership offering an integrated approach to academic support by bringing together key campus learning resources and services for your convenience. Learning Common partners include York Libraries, Learning Skills Services, the Writing Centre, the ESL Open Learning Center, and the Career Centre.
