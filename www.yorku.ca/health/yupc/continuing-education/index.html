ADOS-2 'Booster' Session- This one-day virtual session is designed for professionals who have received previous ADOS-2 training and who would benefit from the opportunity to review administration and coding. This opportunity is ideal for practitioners who may not have administered the ADOS during the pandemic or those who rarely use particular modules. The booster session will review the purpose of the measure, each of the modules and their selection criteria, and the tasks and administration procedures of the modules. This opportunity is open to professionals and graduate students. Participants will be required to follow along with their own manual. Single-use online protocols of each module will be provided to registrants.

ADOS-2 Advanced Scoring Session-This one-day virtual session is designed for professionals who have received previous ADOS-2 training and who use the measure fairly regularly in their practice. The advanced scoring session will involve an in- depth discussion of the nuances of coding, strategies for considering codes, specific details of what behaviour each code is trying to capture, what each number for each code is meant to reflect, and the logic that can be applied to understand the differences between each number on each code. This opportunity is open to professionals and graduate students. Single-use online protocols of each module will be provided to registrants.

EFTT- YT Masterclass Content

Emotion-Focused Skills Training (EFST) for Parents is an evidence-based, innovative, ultra-simple, and quickly effective parent and family support program that facilitates a transformational partnership between families and professionals. EFST supports parents by empowering them as active, primary agents in guiding loved ones of any age through the behavioral, emotional, and relational challenges of everyday living and relating, as well as through the challenges their loved ones face when struggling with a mental health issue, a physical health issue, or any other special circumstances including social and educational challenges.


This Institute will teach and train clinicians in the principles and techniques of delivering EFST for parents as a webinar, a workshop, or on an individual basis. The training places a strong emphasis on experiential learning and will be highly focused on practice change at all levels of experience. It is, therefore, suitable for practitioners new to emotion-focused training as well as for those with basic or advanced levels of training and experience in emotion-focused therapies.


Note that a background in working with families, parents, or children is not required. The structure of EFST that will be taught also has applications to a variety of settings and populations including parents and caregivers, schools, as well as eating disorders, addictions, and more.
The registration fee for this Institute includes one-year access to a 10-hour, online course, allowing clinicians to review and master the core components of EFST for parents. Please note, participation for the full 4 days is required in order to receive the certificate.

The ADOS-2 is a semi-structured, standardized measure of communication, social interaction, play, and restricted and receptive behaviours synonymous with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). It is widely considered the âgold standardâ measure of behavioural observation for assessment and diagnosis of ASD. With updated protocols and the addition of the Toddler Module, the ADOS-2 provides a highly accurate evaluation of current behaviour in individuals from 12 months of age to adulthood, from individuals who are nonverbal to individuals who are verbally fluent. By observing and coding these behaviors, you can obtain information that informs diagnosis, treatment planning, and educational placement.
