Welcome to the Financial Services Department

The Financial Services Department provides a wide range of professional, value added financial services in support of York University's mission and vision. These services include:

  • financial reporting
  • strategic procurement services
  • treasury management
  • accounting
  • investment management over endowment and pension assets
  • information on taxation
  • ensuring legislative compliance
  • risk management services
  • cash management
  • eCommerce
