Orientation Week Events

Have fun and get interactive

At Orientation Week you will familiarize yourself with your new environment while making new friends. Orientation Week is brought to you by your college council.

Each college council has planned a series of unforgettable events specific to your college.

Don't know what college you belong to? No problem. Just click on over to our handy College Finder. Then click on your program of study to find out which college you belong to.

Once you know your college you can click on it below. There you will find:

  • Links to BUY ORIENTATION KITS
  • Links to Orientation Week registration
  • College Council email info
  • Links to your college on social media
