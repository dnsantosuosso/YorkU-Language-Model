Living at York

Housing Services

Living in residence is an incredible opportunity to connect with other students, build community, learn about yourself, and get the most out of your university experience. Residence living fosters academic success, engagement and personal development.

Residence Life

Our Residence Life staff look forward to getting to know you. They provide support, guidance, and various social and educational activities that contribute to a memorable and rewarding residence experience. They are ready to assist with your transition into campus life and to connect you with the campus resources to help you succeed.
