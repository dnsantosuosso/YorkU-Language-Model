STUDENT FAQ

1 - Make sure to check the email you received from York University letting you know when you will have access to YU START. It will not allow you in before your access begins.

2 - The following list of programs are eligible for access to YU START. If you do not find your program on the list then you are not eligible for access to YU START.

Not all students will use the YU START Online Enrolment Tutorial to Enrol in courses. Here is a list of programs that use the Online Enrolment Tutorial. If your program is not on the list then you do not use the Online Enrolment Tutorial. Set up an appointment with your faculty through your MyFile account.

Access to YU Start’s Online Enrollment Tutorial is 72 hours prior to your enrolment access date (found in your MyFile)- so make sure you are trying to complete the Online Enrolment after access is granted. YU Start’s Online Community is open to you as soon as you log in- check out the different sections learn about all the resources available to you at York! If you are still having trouble accessing content, please click here to report the issue.

1 - Ensure you have completed sections 1-5 of the Online Enrolment Tutorial

2 -  If you just completed the Online Enrolment Tutorial, the button already says "Enrol Now", the system may need some time to process your completion. Log out of YU START and try again in 30-60 minutes

3 - Confirm your enrolment access date - if you are trying to enrol prior to your access date you will not be able to do so.

The Pay Enrolment Deposit checkbox will not be updated until early July. If you have paid your enrolment deposit, please go to your Student Account Online Statement (https://sfs.yorku.ca/fees/your-student-account) or your Financial Institution to confirm. Since the deposit is part of your tuition, it will not appear as a separate charge on your student account Statement.

1 -  It's possible you are trying to access YU START before you have access. Please check the email you received from York University telling you when you would have access to YU START.

2 - If you should already have access to YU START, it's possible the system is not finding your email. You must update your email in the 'Student Personal Information' module. The link to this module is here: http://currentstudents.yorku.ca/student-personal-information

Once you have entered your email, the YU START application will update with the email that you have provided. Just make sure to log out and log back in.

If you're still having difficulty logging in, please click here to report the issue.

*Please note that YU START will not be accessible during the hours of 12am-1am EST as it goes under maintenance every night.
