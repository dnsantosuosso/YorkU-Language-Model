Scholarships, Awards, and Bursaries

Schulich students have access to a full range of scholarships and awards which recognize excellence through academic achievement and extracurricular involvement in the School and/or wider community. Through the generosity of alumni and friends, these scholarships and awards are among the highest value offered to business students in Canada and around the world.

Application Process Selection Notification
Entrance Funding:

Incoming domestic students from Schulich Master programs with the exception of Master of Accounting (MAcc) will be invited to apply for entrance funding during the admission process.

International applicants are automatically considered for all merit-based entrance awards and scholarships1. Application is not required.

For those awards that have a financial need component and/or discretionary criteria (e.g. extra-curricular and community involvement, leadership abilities, etc.), invitation will be extended to shortlisted applicants.

OSOTF (Ontario Student Opportunity Trust Fund) Guidelines

Many awards listed indicate that students must satisfy OSOTF residency requirements to apply. The “OSOTF awards” refer to a class of awards, which have resulted from Ontario government’s “matching” program established in the late 1990’s. These awards are restricted to individuals who meet all of the following criteria at the time of application: 1) Canadian citizen, Permanent Resident of Canada or Protected Person; 2) Resident of Ontario as per OSAP guidelines; 3) Demonstrates financial need.

Additional Sources of Funding

In addition to scholarships, awards, and bursaries, students can obtain financial support through provincial loan programs such as Ontario Student Assistance Program, private lenders such as banks, or part-time job opportunities.
