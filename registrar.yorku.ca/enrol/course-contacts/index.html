Course Contact Directory

Course permissions are managed by the department offering the course. Use this contact directory to contact the department if you tried enrolling in a course and received one of these messages on the Registration and Enrolment Module (REM):

  • The spaces in this course are reserved.
  • The course is restricted from dropping.
  • The last day to add without permission from the department has passed.

When emailing a course contact, please include your name, student ID number and the details of the course you are inquiring about (course code, section and term). This contact directory lists courses alphabetically by Faculty, Subject and Department. Some couses are offered by more than one Faculty.

If you are unsure of what course(s) to enrol in, or need clarification on your degree/program requirements, contact your Faculty’s Academic Advising Office.
