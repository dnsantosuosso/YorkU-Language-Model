Employers

Lassonde Co-op/Internship

Employers can post job opportunities, find a student to fill their open positions, and talk to students directly through the Lassonde Co-op Program. Our program helps employers find quality engineering students by providing recruitment services and employer of the year recognition. Take advantage of our support system and partner up with funding organizations to help you with your hiring needs.
