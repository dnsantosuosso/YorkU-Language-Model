Surname rules:

  • If your surname has less than 4 characters just enter your full surname
  • If there is a - or a ' on your surname skip it and do not enter it
  • If your surname has accents, use the unaccented letter.

If you have never used Passport York before, then enter the information on the left to create your Passport York username and password.

Once you have created your username and password, you can use them to sign in at Passport York login pages, such as yu link, Maximo and using AirYork wireless internet on campus.
