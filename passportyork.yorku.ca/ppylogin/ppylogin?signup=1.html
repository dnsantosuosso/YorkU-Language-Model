Passport York Sign-Up for New Students

Note: You can only use the New Student Sign-Up page once.



Passport York authenticates you as a member of the York community and gives you access to a wide range of computing resources and services.

If you have never used Passport York before, then enter the information on the left to create your Passport York username and password.

Once you have created your username and password, you can use them to sign in at Passport York login pages, such as registering for courses, checking your grades, MOODLE and using AirYork wireless internet on campus.

If you need help, please contact us at http://student.computing.yorku.ca/uit-service-desk-counter/
