STUDENT SUCCESS ADVISING

Welcome to Student Services & International Relations! Our team is dedicated to providing a wealth of one-to-one advising & drop-in appointments for a variety of academic and non-academic student inquiries.

We welcome you to join us with your questions about courses, enrolment, specializations, and your academic success! Either attend in-person drop-in advising, or book an appointment on the calendar below!

Important Update

Option 1: One-on-One Appointment

Please scroll down and complete the Appointment Info section and the Personal Details section below.
You will be able to select your appointment date, time, and the location, based on the availability of that time.

Once you’ve completed both sections, please click Submit.

You will receive an automated confirmation e-mail once you book your appointment.  If you have opted for a Zoom meeting, your dedicated Advisor will provide the details to you within 24 hours of your appointment.

Option 2: In-Person Drop-in

Please visit the Student Services & International Relations Office (W263 – Schulich School of Business, Keele Campus) on the following day and time:

Every Tuesday from 10am to 1pm

In-person drop-in on Tuesday, October 24 will be from 10am to 12pm.

Please note that each visiting student will be attended to on a first-come, first-serve basis.

INTERNATIONAL STUDENT ADVISING

International students can book a one-on-one appointment with our International Student Support Specialist for immigration inquiries, travel and transition to Canada, or other international student concerns.

You can also attend the online Q&A Drop-In Sessions for International Students.

To contact the International Relations Office directly, email intlrelations@schulich.yorku.ca.

PETITIONS/ACADEMIC HONESTY

Students seeking guidance on academic petitions, appeals, and academic integrity details are invited to schedule an appointment with the Student Academic Services Coordinator.
