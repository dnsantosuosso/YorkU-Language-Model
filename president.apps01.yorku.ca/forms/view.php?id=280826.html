National Day for Truth and Reconciliation at York University: Film Screenings

In honour of the National Day of Truth and Reconciliation, The Division of Equity, People & Culture along with a variety of York University partners is hosting a series of film screenings on both the Keele and Glendon campuses. We encourage community members to engage with these media to support their learning on a path to reconciliation. We hope that these films will help you understand the impact of Residential Schools on Indigenous peoples and communities.
